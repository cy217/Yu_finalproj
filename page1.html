Welcome!

This project aims to enhance historical understanding by following a narrative through time, incorporating personal narrative and photographs. By using multimedia material from a variety of archives, users will be able to use this digital library engage more fully and visualize the world in which the material was formed. As you browse our collection, please think about how it connects to modern day thoughts on war, immigration, and who exactly is considered a citizen in the United States.

To explore what we have to offer click The Project, and to learn more about us click on Additional Information.
